lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, meat or vegetable sauce, béchamel (white sauce) or ricotta cheese, and melted cheese (usually mozzarella and Parmesan). It is baked in the oven until golden and bubbly. Traditional versions, like lasagna alla bolognese, use a rich ragù sauce, while variations include vegetarian or seafood options.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped (optional)
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil (or fresh basil leaves)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 125ml red wine (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil – In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté vegetables – Add chopped onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until soft.
  3. Cook the meat – Add minced beef (or beef & pork mix), breaking it up as it browns.
  4. Add tomato base – Stir in tomato purée, chopped tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer – Pour in red wine (if using) and let it reduce. Simmer for 20–30 minutes until thickened.
